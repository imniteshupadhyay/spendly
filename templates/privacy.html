Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you register, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. You can delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond as promptly as possible.
